🚀 Featured Project

Battlefield Medical French Speech Recognition
PyTorch ONNX Raspberry Pi Whisper Speech Recognition Embedded AI

Master's Thesis · 2024-2025

Real-time French medical speech recognition system designed for drone deployment in battlefield environments. Achieves state-of-the-art performance in extreme noise conditions.

📊 Key Results

  • Built 50,000-sample dataset with 82.8% real drone noise
  • Achieved 7.0% WER (21.7% improvement over baseline)
  • 14 out of 21 SNR levels reached 100% accuracy
  • Deployed on Raspberry Pi 4B with <3s inference time
  • 23x improvement in keyword recognition

💡 Key Finding

Training on drone noise improved performance on ALL noise types:

  • Helicopter noise: +7.05% improvement
  • Weapon noise: +5.34% improvement
  • Radio noise: +3.97% improvement
